Shelfswap is a free community for swapping books with people near you. No fees. No algorithms. Just books finding new homes.

📚
List in seconds

Type a title or scan an ISBN. Your book is live instantly for anyone nearby to find.

🤝
Local swaps

Meet locally or post. Every swap is between real people — no middleman, no commission.

🇬🇧
UK only

Focused on the UK so swaps are practical. Your local community, not the whole internet.

♾️
Always free

No listing fees, no subscription, no ads. Shelfswap is free because books should be shared.

How it works

1

Find a book

Browse what's available near you. Enter your postcode to see books within miles of you.

2

Request a swap

Tap a book and send a swap request. The owner gets an email notification straight away.

3

Meet or post

When the owner accepts, contact details are shared. Arrange a local meetup or post the book.
